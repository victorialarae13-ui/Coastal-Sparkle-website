FAQ

Do I need to be home? +
Nope! Many clients provide a key or entry code. We'll take great care of your home whether you're there or not.
Do you bring your own supplies? +
Yes! We bring everything needed. If you have a preference for specific products, just let us know in advance.
What areas do you serve? +
We serve the Central Coast of California including San Luis Obispo, Santa Barbara, Santa Maria, Paso Robles, Pismo Beach, Morro Bay, Arroyo Grande, and surrounding areas.
How do I book? +
Easy! Click the "Book Now" button to schedule through Calendly, or call/text us directly at (805) 530-7868.
How much do you charge? +
Pricing depends on the type and size of your home. Check out our Services page for detailed pricing, or reach out for a custom quote!
